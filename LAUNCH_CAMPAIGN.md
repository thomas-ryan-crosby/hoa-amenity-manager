## 4. Execution Steps

### Week 1: Build List + Prep (April 14–18)

- [ ] **Build prospect list** — 200 contacts minimum
  - Source 1: Google "[city] HOA management company" for each metro
  - Source 2: CAI Louisiana chapter member directory
  - Source 3: LinkedIn Sales Navigator — "property manager" + "Louisiana"
  - Source 4: Community websites — find board member names/emails
  - Source 5: DSLD Homes community map → identify HOAs they built
- [ ] **Segment list into tiers:**
  - Tier 1: PM companies (50 contacts)
  - Tier 2: Luxury community boards (100 contacts)
  - Tier 3: Builder HOA contacts (50 contacts)
- [ ] **Set up sending infrastructure:**
  - Use a tool like Instantly.ai, Smartlead, or Apollo.io for sequencing
  - Warm up your sending domain (send from thomas@neighbri.com or outreach@neighbri.com)
  - Set up a separate sending domain (e.g., neighbri.io) to protect neighbri.com's reputation
- [ ] **Create landing page variant** for Louisiana campaign
  - Could be neighbri.com/louisiana with Sanctuary testimonial prominently featured

### Week 2: Launch Northshore + GNO (April 21–25)

- [ ] **Send Email 1** to Tier 1 (PM companies) — 50 contacts
- [ ] **Send Email 1** to Tier 2 Northshore communities — 30 contacts
- [ ] **Personalize** each email with community name and specific amenities
- [ ] **Monitor** opens, clicks, replies daily
- [ ] **Reply to every response** within 2 hours during business hours
- [ ] **Follow up with Email 2** on Day 4 to non-responders

### Week 3: Expand to Baton Rouge + Lafayette (April 28–May 2)

- [ ] **Send Email 1** to Tier 2 BR + Lafayette communities — 50 contacts
- [ ] **Send Email 1** to Tier 3 (builder contacts) — 50 contacts
- [ ] **Continue sequence** for Week 2 contacts (Emails 3, 4)
- [ ] **Book demos** for all positive replies
- [ ] **CAI Louisiana outreach** — contact chapter for upcoming events, inquire about sponsorship/speaking opportunity

### Week 4: Follow-Up + Close (May 5–9)

- [ ] **Send breakup emails** to non-responders
- [ ] **Conduct demos** for all booked meetings
- [ ] **Onboard first 3–5 new communities** (hands-on white-glove setup)
- [ ] **Ask The Sanctuary for a testimonial** (written quote or short video)
- [ ] **Post on LinkedIn** about the launch — tag The Sanctuary if they're okay with it

### Ongoing: Networking + Content

- [ ] **Attend CAI Louisiana chapter events** — bring demo on laptop
- [ ] **Connect with HOA attorneys** — they refer management companies and communities
- [ ] **Create case study** — "How The Sanctuary eliminated amenity booking headaches"
- [ ] **LinkedIn content** — post 2x/week about HOA amenity management tips
